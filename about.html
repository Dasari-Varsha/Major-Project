ABOUT

Menstrual Health Chatbot aims to bridge that gap by offering a supportive, informative, and easily accessible resource for anyone seeking to better understand and manage their menstrual cycle. Through friendly, AI-driven conversations, the chatbot addresses common concerns, tracks symptoms, and provides guidance for day-to-day care.

This chatbot delivers reliable information and suggestions to help you stay prepared and comfortable. By logging daily experiences, users can gain deeper insights into patterns and triggers, ultimately promoting a more proactive approach to menstrual health..

SheCares Chatbot encourages overall wellness by offering stress management strategies, nutritional advice, and emotional support. It is designed not only to inform but also to empower users to make confident decisions about their bodies.
